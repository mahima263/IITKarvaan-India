IIT Karvaan: Celebrating Alumni Connections Across Cities

IIT Karvaan is making its way to your city, and we’re thrilled to invite you to an exclusive IITK Alumni Meet! This event is a perfect opportunity to reconnect with fellow alumni, relive cherished memories from your time at IIT Kanpur, and expand your professional network. Whether you graduated recently or many years ago, IIT Karvaan celebrates the unbreakable bond that ties us all together.
