IIT Karvaan: Celebrating Alumni Connections Across Cities

The journey of IIT Karvaan continues, bringing the spirit and energy of IIT Kanpur to cities across the country!

Through every edition, Karvaan has kept the IITK community connected - reviving memories, friendships, and the spirit that unites us all.

Join fellow alumni from across all batches to reconnect, share stories, and relive the moments that shaped your IIT Kanpur years. Hear familiar tales of halls, classrooms, and campus corners - told anew by different generations and rediscover how the IITK experience transcends time.

Whether you graduated recently or decades ago, IIT Karvaan celebrates the unbreakable bond of curiosity, camaraderie, and a shared pursuit of excellence, that connects every member of the IITK family.
